Brennan's Best Movies (a non comprhensive list from a non movie goer)

My top 3 movies of near-time

The Road to El Dorado

I've loved this film since I was a child. The plot is fantastic and inspiring, and the characters have such humor between them!
The OST is also one of my favorites; the music is written by Elton John, and it ties in well with the themes and imagery of the film!

Inception

This movie is a real mind-fuck. The concept fascinates me, and the cinematography is top notch.
I'll always be questioning the ending of Inception! There are so many little things to analyze that hint at the truth...

Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story

This is my most quoted movie by FAR. I'm a musician, and this movie hits close to the heart on so many levels!
I can still watch Walk Hard and laugh at every joke, even though I know them all!
